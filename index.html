Registration

Registration is now open for PhD students and Postdocs.
The school is funded by the AHEAD project and it is free of charge, but the number of participants is limited to 20.
Therefore, every applicant must submit:

  • an up to date Curriculum Vitae
  • a cover letter by the applicant, detailing their motivation to participate in this school
  • a letter of recommendation from the applicant's supervisor (in case the applicant is a PhD student)

    • The Scientific Organizing Committee will evaluate all the applications received within XX.YY.2018, when the registration will be closed.
